Meet the Artist

My passion for art began at a very early age. And I was also influenced by my grandfather who was a practicing artist and was a frequent member of various art galleries. You could say the passion runs in the family! I fell in love with art at the tender age of 9 and started honing my craft from the age of 15. I have studied and copied various old masters; and I aim to achieve a likeness similar to the Hudson River painters. I have always had an inclination towards landscapes. Something about the beauty and chaos in nature always resonated with me. I hope I can share a more about my individual paintings through this blog. And I hope you enjoy and resonate with them just as I do.
